Welcome to Assam Veterinary and Fishery University

Assam Veterinary and Fisheries University (AVFU) is a State University established by the Government of Assam under The Assam Veterinary and Fishery University Act, 2024 (Assam Act No. XXI of 2024). Headquartered at Khanapara, Guwahati, the University was constituted in February 2024 through the integration of veterinary and fisheries institutions of the State. AVFU is dedicated to education, research, extension, and technology transfer in veterinary, animal, fishery, and allied sciences, with the objective of supporting sustainable livestock and fisheries development in Assam.
